About me

My journey into computer science started seriously in a pretty unconventional way — by creating jokes! My first “job” was designing a harmless troll virus. It earned me my very first paycheck! That experience showed me the power of programming.

Later on, I made a game starring my teacher as the main character — a project that truly sparked my curiosity and passion for coding. I have many stories connected with IT — enough to fill a book. The most important lesson I learned back then is that I can teach myself anything, as long as I make it interesting.
